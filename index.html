How to Play

To play, twist the cube's sides to mix up the colors. The goal is to solve the cube so that each face has a single color. You can use algorithms and strategies to solve the cube layer by layer or by other methods, aiming for the fastest time!

About Rubik's Cube

Rubik's Cube is a 3D combination puzzle invented in 1974 by Ernő Rubik. It's a widely popular puzzle that challenges players to solve it by twisting and turning its sides to align the colors on each face.
